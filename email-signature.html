How to add this signature in Zoho Mail

  1. Open Zoho Mail → click the gear icon ⚙ (top-right) → Settings.
  2. In the left sidebar click Signatures.
  3. Click "Add Signature", give it a name e.g. PeptidePro.
  4. In the editor toolbar click the </> Source button to switch to HTML mode.
  5. Click "Copy Signature HTML" below, then paste into the source editor.
  6. Click Save, then click "Set as Default" next to the signature.
Before saving: replace the four href="#" in the social buttons with your actual profile URLs.
